# Negotiation Preparation Checklist

Use this checklist before entering any negotiation.

---

## Part 1: Perception Check

### About Yourself
- [ ] Have I clearly defined what I want?
- [ ] Have I examined WHY I want it?
- [ ] Am I aware of my biases?

### About Them
- [ ] Have I genuinely tried to see their perspective?
- [ ] Am I making assumptions about their intentions?
- [ ] What might they know that I don't?

---

## Part 2: Emotion Check

### Pre-Negotiation
- [ ] Am I emotionally ready for this conversation?
- [ ] Have I identified what might trigger me?
- [ ] Do I have strategies to stay calm?

### Emotional Awareness
- [ ] What am I feeling right now?
- [ ] How might my emotions affect my judgment?
- [ ] What emotions might they be bringing?

---

## Part 3: Communication Check

### Listening Preparation
- [ ] Am I prepared to listen more than talk?
- [ ] Have I prepared clarifying questions?
- [ ] Am I ready to paraphrase to confirm understanding?

### Speaking Preparation
- [ ] Can I separate the person from the problem?
- [ ] Am I ready to use "I" statements?
- [ ] Do I have data to support my positions?

---

## Part 4: Strategic Preparation

### Your Position
**Target Outcome:** _________________________________

**Reservation Point (walk-away):** _________________________________

**BATNA:** _________________________________

### Their Position
**Their Likely Target:** _________________________________

**Their Likely Reservation:** _________________________________

**Their Likely BATNA:** _________________________________

### ZOPA Analysis
Is there overlap between reservation points? Yes / No / Uncertain

---

## Part 5: Research

- [ ] Market rates/prices gathered
- [ ] Industry standards documented
- [ ] Comparable deals researched
- [ ] Recent news about other party reviewed

## Quick Pre-Meeting Checklist

- [ ] Take 3 deep breaths
- [ ] Review your BATNA
- [ ] Remember your key interests
- [ ] Commit to listening first
- [ ] Set intention: curious, not defensive
