# Research Templates by Negotiation Type

WebSearch strategies to gather data for different negotiations.

## Salary/Compensation

**Search queries:**
- `[job title] salary [location] [year]`
- `[company name] compensation glassdoor`
- `[industry] [role] total compensation range`
- `cost of living [city] vs [city]`

**Key data points:**
- Base salary ranges (25th, 50th, 75th percentile)
- Total compensation (bonus, equity, benefits)
- Company-specific pay philosophy
- Recent funding/financial health

**Sources to cite:** Glassdoor, Levels.fyi, LinkedIn Salary, Payscale, industry surveys

## Real Estate

**Search queries:**
- `[address or neighborhood] recent sales`
- `[zip code] median home price [year]`
- `[neighborhood] days on market average`
- `[seller name or company] motivation news`

**Key data points:**
- Comparable sales (last 6 months)
- Days on market vs area average
- Price per square foot
- Recent price reductions
- Seller motivation (relocation, divorce, financial)

**Sources to cite:** Zillow, Redfin, county records, MLS data

## Vendor/Contract

**Search queries:**
- `[product/service] pricing comparison`
- `[vendor name] competitors alternatives`
- `[product category] enterprise pricing benchmarks`
- `[vendor name] contract negotiation tips`

**Key data points:**
- Competitor pricing
- Standard contract terms
- Volume discounts typical in industry
- Vendor's customer concentration/dependency

**Sources to cite:** G2, Capterra, industry reports, vendor SEC filings

## Job Offer

**Search queries:**
- `[company] offer negotiation success`
- `[company] hiring process timeline`
- `[industry] signing bonus typical`
- `[company] stock refresh equity grants`

**Key data points:**
- Company's negotiation reputation
- Typical offer components
- Competing companies' packages
- Company's current hiring urgency

## Dispute/Settlement

**Search queries:**
- `[type of dispute] settlement amounts`
- `[industry] mediation outcomes`
- `[jurisdiction] small claims process`
- `similar case settlement precedent`

**Key data points:**
- Typical settlement ranges
- Cost of litigation vs settlement
- Precedent cases
- Mediation success rates
